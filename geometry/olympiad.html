Olympiad Geometry

This page aims to collect my solutions to geometry problems from various national and international math olympiads.

IMO Shortlist Geometry Problems

One of my favourite collection of problems has been the IMO Shortlist, a set of very high quality shortlisted problems from a dozen of proposals received by the IMO jury. My aim is to ponder over each of these problems and collect as many approaches as I can to these problems!
