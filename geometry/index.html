Geometry Corner

Geometry has been one area of mathematics that has fascinated me for years because of its beauty. As a kid, I loved solving olympiad geometry problems, and even now as a software engineer, I still find myself drawn to reading about the history of geometry and the branches of mathematics that evolved from it. This corner of the blog will collect the things I find interesting in this area.

A sidenote: it is much easier for me to write geometry posts in LaTeX and attach the PDFs here than to write them directly in markdown. I plan to explore better ways to create beautiful markdown geometry posts that can be deployed immediately, but for now I'll stick to attaching PDFs.
